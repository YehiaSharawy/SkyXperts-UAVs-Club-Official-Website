SkyXperts UAVs Club

A project by students of the Arab Academy for Science and Technology

GOALS & OBJECTIVES

Our primary objective is to manufacture an Quadcopter, a versatile and robust UAV platform capable of performing a wide range of tasks and to become a leading UAV team in Egypt, making significant contributions to the development of UAV technology and inspiring others to pursue their dreams in STEM fields.

Objective

Our goal is to participate in 3 international competitions to show our skills and experience. We seek to participate in SUAS for Quadcopters, SAE for Fixed Wings, and Mate ROV for rovs.
